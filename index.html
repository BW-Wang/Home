Biography

Bowen Wang received the B.S. degree (Hons.) in communication engineering in 2021 from School of Information Engineering, Wuhan University of Technology (WUT), Wuhan, China. He is currently pursuing the M.S. degree in communication engineering, School of Information and Communication Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China.

His research interests include dual-function radar-communication systems, intelligent reflecting surface, radar signal processing, millimeter-wave communications, one-bit signal processing, and mathematical optimization.
